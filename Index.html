Our Educational Sections

We offer a comprehensive range of educational programs to cater to all ages and levels of Islamic learning.

Mosque & Majlis

Our community hub for daily prayers, Jumu'ah, Islamic lectures, and spiritual gatherings. Open to all members of the community.

Learn More

Tahfiz Section

Dedicated program for the memorization (Hifz) of the Holy Quran. We focus on Tajweed and retention for lifelong connection with the Quran.

Learn More

Islamiyya

Foundational Islamic studies covering Aqeedah (creed), Fiqh (jurisprudence), Seerah (prophetic biography), and Islamic manners.

Learn More

Higher Islamic

Advanced studies for older students and adults. Delve deeper into Islamic sciences, theology, and contemporary issues.

Learn More

Primary School

Blending the national curriculum with core Islamic values. We provide a strong academic foundation in a faith-based environment.

Learn More

Nursery

A safe, loving, and stimulating environment for our youngest learners. Introduction to basic Islamic principles through play.
